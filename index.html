Featured Speakers

Faker

Professional League of Legends Player

Lee Sang-Hyeok, better known as Faker, is a South Korean professional League of Legends player. Formerly known as GoJeonPa on the Korean server, he was hired by LCK's SK Telecom in 2013 and has since played as the team's center lane.

Bjergsen

Professional League of Legends Player

Søren Bjerg, better known by his in-game name Bjergsen, is a Danish former professional League of Legends mid-lane player. Currently he is the head coach and part-owner of Team SoloMid of the League of Legends Championship Series.

JJonak

Professional Overwatch Player

Bang Sung-hyeon, better known by his online alias JJonak, is a South Korean professional esports player, best known as a competitive Overwatch player for New York Excelsior. He was named most valuable player of the league's first season.

N0tail

Professional Dota 2 Player

Johan Sundstein, better known as N0tail, is a Danish professional Dota 2 player and captain for OG. With them, he has played in four iterations of The International, winning in 2018 and 2019, and has also won four Major championships.

Firebat

Professional Hearthstone Player

James Kostesich, better known as Firebat, was absolutely crushing Hearthstone esports in the first couple of years of its existence and has stayed in the game since, streaming and giving advice and representing team Omnislash.

Coldzera

Professional Counter Strike Player

Marcelo Augusto David, better known as coldzera, is a Brazilian professional Counter Strike: Global Offensive player for FaZe Clan. He was named the best CS:GO player in both 2016 and 2017 by the CS:GO ranking website HLTV.org.
