eSports Global Conference 2020

The Global Conference brings to everyone a unique experience every year.
This 2020 we invited the best players among the most played games around the world to one place at the same time. We also share knowledge on other topics aswell, like souvenirs vendors with everything related to geeks like us, or the latest card expansions from Magic: The Gathering.
And of course we can't miss the professional tech service to ensure you can make your own computer with the best hardware to run all of the games we will have on the conference. If you want to meet the pros of the pros, this is your time, don't lose it!

Any questions? Share them with us on eSportsconf@ esports.com, we will be happy to help you.

For the new experiences seekers!

This year we will be introducing the hyped videogame Genshin Impact. We will have two of the main developers with us sharing ideas and describing how awesome it was to build this amazing game. We're looking forward to bring new experiences to all our guests for the incoming years!
